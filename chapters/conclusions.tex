\section{Conclusion}

This report provided a comparison of two models on a specific dataset containing
coffee leaf disease images. The Simple CNN model gave the best test performance results with
95.88\% accuracy. MobileNetV2 achieved the second-best test accuracy, with 94.43\%.
Moreover, MobileNetV2 also showed high F1-scores. It is worth mentioning that the
MobileNetV2 model was trained much faster as only the classifier was trained on the
pre-trained base model. Therefore, the result did not show that one model was always
the best, but that each model had its own advantages. The Simple CNN model gave better results in
this notebook run, but MobileNetV2 was more efficient during training.

Both models were used for the comparison. On the other hand, some limitations were also
taken into account. Thus, among the technical errors, there were the use of incorrect MobileNetV2
preprocessing, lack of evaluation of the fine-tuned MobileNetV2 model separately, and
possibility of data leakage due to image augmentations.
